Our Mission




Empowering Filipino Palates, Nurturing Local Roots At CEBUTA Homemade Jam, our mission is to celebrate the rich tapestry of Filipino flavors by handcrafting exceptional homemade jams using the finest locally-sourced ingredients. We are committed to preserving tradition while fostering innovation, delivering a taste of home in every jar. With every spoonful, we aim to cultivate a deeper connection to our culinary heritage, supporting local communities and promoting sustainable practices.

A Taste of Tradition, A Future of Flavor We envision a future where our homemade jams become a cherished staple in Filipino households, igniting a passion for authentic, locally-made delicacies. Through our dedication to quality, creativity, and community, we strive to be the go-to source for discerning palates seeking a taste of homegrown excellence. Together, we are redefining the essence of Filipino flavor, one jar at a time.





Our Story





Our Story At CEBUTA Homemade Jam, our story is steeped in the lush landscapes and vibrant flavors of the Philippines. We are more than just a homemade jam company; we are a celebration of Filipino tradition, a testament to the bountiful harvests of our land, and a labor of love that began in our very own kitchen
Rooted in Tradition Our journey started with a simple idea: to capture the essence of our beloved homeland in a jar. We set out to preserve the age-old recipes that have been passed down through generations, paying homage to the tastes that have graced Filipino dining tables for decades. Our commitment to tradition is what sets us apart; we honor the culinary heritage of the Philippines in every batch we create.
Locally Sourced, Lovingly Crafted What truly makes our homemade jams exceptional is our dedication to using the finest, locally-sourced ingredients. From the sweetest mangoes in Cebu to the lushest strawberries in Baguio, we carefully select our fruits to ensure that every spoonful tells a story of the Philippines' diverse and bountiful produce.
Crafted with Care Every jar of CEBUTA homemade jam is a labor of love. Our team of passionate jam-makers painstakingly cook, stir, and bottle each batch by hand, ensuring that the flavors are preserved and the quality is unrivaled. It's not just about making jam; it's about creating an experience, a taste that transports you to the heart of the Philippines.
Community and Sustainability Our commitment to our homeland goes beyond the kitchen. We believe in giving back to the communities that inspire us. We work closely with local farmers and artisans, supporting their livelihoods and promoting sustainability. When you choose CEBUTA Homemade Jam, you're not just enjoying a delicious treat; you're investing in the future of Filipino agriculture.
Join Us on this Flavorful Journey We invite you to join us on this flavorful journey through the Philippines. With every jar of our homemade jam, you're not just savoring a taste; you're savoring a piece of our heritage, a piece of our hearts. Thank you for being a part of our story, and we can't wait to be a part of yours.
